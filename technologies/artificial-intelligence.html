What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans. AI can perform tasks such as recognising speech, making decisions, and solving problems, often with greater accuracy and efficiency than human beings. It encompasses various technologies, including machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision, which enable machines to process and analyse vast amounts of data to make informed decisions.
