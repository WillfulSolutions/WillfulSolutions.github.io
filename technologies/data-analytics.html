What is Data Analytics?

Data Analytics involves examining datasets to draw conclusions about the information they contain, using various techniques and tools. It encompasses processes such as data collection, data cleansing, data transformation, and data modelling. By applying statistical analysis, predictive modelling, and machine learning, Data Analytics helps organisations uncover patterns, trends, and insights to make informed decisions, optimise operations, and drive strategic initiatives. The ultimate goal of Data Analytics is to transform raw data into actionable intelligence for better business outcomes.
